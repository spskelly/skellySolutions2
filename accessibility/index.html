My Commitment to Accessibility

At Skelly Solutions, I am dedicated to ensuring that digital platforms are accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. My one-man team approach allows me to offer personalized consulting services to help your business meet Section 508 and the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) standards for digital accessibility.

My Compliance Services

  • Accessibility Audits: Personalized evaluations of your digital assets to identify compliance gaps.
  • Remediation Plans: Tailored strategies and support to address and correct identified accessibility issues.
  • Training and Support: Customized training sessions for you and your team on implementing and maintaining accessibility standards.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Ongoing support to ensure that new and updated content adheres to accessibility standards.
